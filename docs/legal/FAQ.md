## General Questions

### What is Second Brain RAG System?
Second Brain is an AI-powered knowledge management system that helps you organize, search, and interact with your documents using natural language. Upload your files, ask questions, and get instant answers backed by your own content.

### Who develops Second Brain?
Second Brain is developed and maintained by **Alien Probe Reports**, a technology company focused on AI-powered productivity tools.

### What does "RAG" mean?
RAG stands for Retrieval-Augmented Generation. It's an AI technique that combines document search with language generation to provide accurate, context-aware answers based on your specific content.

### Is Second Brain suitable for businesses?
Yes! Second Brain is designed for both individual users and teams. We offer scalable plans for businesses of all sizes, from startups to enterprises.

## Getting Started

### How do I create an account?
1. Visit [secondbrain.alienprobeports.com](https://secondbrain.alienprobeports.com)
2. Click "Sign in with Google"
3. Authorize the application
4. Choose your subscription plan
5. Start uploading documents!

No password required—we use secure Google OAuth authentication.

### What types of files can I upload?
Second Brain supports a wide range of file formats:
- **Documents**: PDF, DOCX, TXT, Markdown
- **Transcripts**: VTT, SRT subtitle files
- **Images**: PNG, JPG (with OCR text extraction)
- **Web Content**: URLs from websites and YouTube videos

### Is there a file size limit?
- Free tier: 10MB per file, 100MB total storage
- Pro tier: 50MB per file, 10GB total storage  
- Enterprise: Custom limits available

### How many documents can I upload?
- Free tier: Up to 100 documents
- Pro tier: Up to 10,000 documents
- Enterprise: Unlimited documents

## Using the Service

### How do I ask questions?
1. Navigate to the "Ask" tab
2. Type your question in natural language
3. Click "Ask" or press Enter
4. View your AI-generated answer with source citations

You can ask anything related to your uploaded content!

### How accurate are the answers?
Answers are generated based on your uploaded documents using advanced AI models (OpenAI GPT-4, Claude). Accuracy depends on:
- Quality and completeness of your source documents
- Clarity of your question
- Relevance of retrieved content

Always review answers for critical use cases.

### Can I see which documents were used for an answer?
Yes! Every answer includes:
- Source document citations
- Relevant chunks/excerpts from your documents
- Confidence scores
- "View Source" links to the original content

### What languages are supported?
Currently, Second Brain works best with English content. We're actively working on expanding language support.

## Privacy and Security

### Is my data secure?
Yes. We implement industry-standard security measures:
- All data encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest
- Secure authentication via Google OAuth (no passwords stored)
- API keys are hashed and salted
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- SOC 2 Type II compliance (in progress)

### Who can see my documents?
Only you. Your documents are private and isolated to your account. We do not:
- Share your documents with other users
- Use your content to train AI models
- Access your content except for providing the service or responding to support requests

### Do you train AI models on my data?
**No.** Your documents are used solely to provide answers to your questions. We do not use your content to train our AI models or any third-party models.

### Where is my data stored?
Data is stored in secure, encrypted databases hosted on AWS/Google Cloud infrastructure in the United States. Enterprise customers can request regional hosting.

### Can I delete my data?
Yes. You can:
- Delete individual documents anytime via the interface
- Request full account deletion via support
- Data is permanently purged within 30 days of deletion request

## Troubleshooting

### My uploads are failing. What should I do?
Check that your files:
- Are under the size limit for your plan
- Are in a supported format
- Contain readable text (not just images)

If issues persist, contact support with the file name and error message.

### My questions aren't getting good answers. How can I improve results?
Tips for better answers:
- Upload comprehensive, well-organized documents
- Ask specific, clear questions
- Use relevant keywords from your documents
- Break complex questions into multiple simpler ones
