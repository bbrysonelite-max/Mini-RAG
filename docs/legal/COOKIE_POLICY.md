## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

---

## Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function properly:

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `session` | Maintains your login session | Session |
| `csrf_token` | Protects against cross-site request forgery | Session |

### Functional Cookies (Optional)

These cookies remember your preferences:

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `theme` | Remembers dark/light mode preference | 1 year |
| `workspace` | Remembers your last selected workspace | 30 days |

### Analytics Cookies (Optional)

We do not use third-party analytics cookies. All analytics are processed server-side and do not track you across websites.

## Managing Cookies

### Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
- **Firefox**: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
- **Edge**: Settings > Privacy > Cookies

### Disabling Cookies

If you disable essential cookies, you may not be able to:
- Stay logged in
- Use secure features
- Access your documents
