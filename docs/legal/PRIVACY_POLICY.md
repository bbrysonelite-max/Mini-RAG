## Introduction

Alien Probe Reports ("we," "our," or "us") operates Second Brain RAG System (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, and Google account details when you sign in via Google OAuth
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the Service, including queries, documents uploaded, and feature usage
- **Payment Information**: Billing details processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card information)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Browser type, IP address, operating system
- **Cookies and Tracking**: Session cookies for authentication and analytics cookies (opt-in)
- **Log Data**: Server logs including access times, pages viewed, and error reports

### Content You Provide
- **Documents**: Files and URLs you upload for ingestion into your knowledge base
- **Queries**: Questions you ask the RAG system
- **Custom Data**: Any other content you voluntarily provide through the Service

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide the Service**: Process your queries, ingest documents, and generate answers
- **Improve the Service**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and performance
- **Communicate**: Send service updates, security alerts, and respond to support requests
- **Process Payments**: Handle billing and subscription management via Stripe
- **Ensure Security**: Monitor for fraud, abuse, and security threats
- **Comply with Legal Obligations**: Meet regulatory requirements and respond to lawful requests

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage
- Your data is stored in secure, encrypted databases hosted on industry-standard cloud infrastructure
- Documents and embeddings are retained as long as your account is active
- Backups are maintained for disaster recovery purposes

### Security Measures
- All data transmission uses TLS encryption (HTTPS)
- Passwords are never stored (OAuth authentication only)
- API keys are hashed and salted
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- Access controls and authentication on all endpoints

### Data Retention
- Active account data: Retained indefinitely while your subscription is active
- Deleted accounts: Data purged within 30 days of account deletion
- Backups: Retained for 90 days for recovery purposes
- Logs: Retained for 12 months for security and compliance

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do **not** sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: Trusted third parties (e.g., Stripe for payments, OpenAI for embeddings) under strict confidentiality agreements
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law, court order, or government request
- **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with notice to you)
- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information

### Third-Party Services
- **Google OAuth**: For authentication (governed by Google's privacy policy)
- **Stripe**: For payment processing (governed by Stripe's privacy policy)
- **OpenAI/Anthropic**: For AI embeddings and completions (API calls only, your data is not used for model training)
