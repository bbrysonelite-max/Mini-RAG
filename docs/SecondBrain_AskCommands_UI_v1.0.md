# Second Brain – Ask Command UI (Phase I)

TAGS: project:SecondBrain, type:UI, section:AskCommands, version:1.0

---

## 1. Dropdown Label

- Label text: `Command`

- Helper text (small under label):  

  `Choose what you want your Second Brain to do with this message.`

---

## 2. Command Options (Phase I)

Each item shows a label and (on hover) a tooltip.

1. **Ask**  

   - Tooltip: `Ask your Second Brain anything about this workspace.`

2. **Success Coach**  

   - Tooltip: `Get a simple, realistic plan and today's top 3 actions.`

3. **Workflow Agent**  

   - Tooltip: `Turn a messy process into a clear workflow with steps.`

4. **Lead & Social Agent**  

   - Tooltip: `Plan how to find leads and create social content for them.`

5. **Build Prompt**  

   - Tooltip: `Design a reusable prompt you can save and use again.`

6. **Build Workflow**  

   - Tooltip: `Create a workflow with GOAL, TRIGGER, and step-by-step actions.`

7. **Landing Page**  

   - Tooltip: `Draft a landing page with hero, problem, solution, and CTA.`

8. **Email Sequence**  

   - Tooltip: `Generate a multi-email sequence for this offer or lead magnet.`

9. **Content Batch (Social Posts)**  

   - Tooltip: `Create a batch of social posts (e.g., 10 LinkedIn or Instagram posts).`

10. **Decision Record**  

    - Tooltip: `Log an important business decision with options and rationale.`

11. **Build Expert Instructions**  

    - Tooltip: `Research top experts and synthesize a formula or checklist to follow.`

12. **Build Customer Avatar**  

    - Tooltip: `Create an Ideal Customer Avatar for this workspace.`

13. **Pack for Gumroad**  

    - Tooltip: `Bundle existing assets into a product structure and product page copy.`

---
